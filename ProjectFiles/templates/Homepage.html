iCare Pet Services

iCare Pet Services is a mobile application and web application that records the data of the pet owners into the database.It keeps the records of the services that have been provided such as vaccination, deworming, vet assistance, pet breeding, and anti-flea.


Trusted care for your pet

Any pet owners need to cater care and needs for their pets. We have noticed the struggles in this process by uneasy services in gathering the data and giving services for the pets in any agencies, businesses, and barangays. We are fixing the usual way that was in all the data of a pet where recorded in a piece of papers or envelopes. So, introducing iCare Pet Services. Our system caters to pets in vaccination, deworming, vet assistance, pet breeding, and anti-flea. It keeps the records of the pet owner s information in the database. Their pets will be categorized depending on the type of animal and will have unique identification for information access. Pet Owners have no limits on the number of pets being registered into the database if the pet is still alive.
